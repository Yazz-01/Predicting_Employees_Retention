Ecobici

Sustainable transport inside Mexico City


Project Summary

Why this matters: Since its launch in 2010, Ecobici have provided over 71 million rides in central Mexico City: convenient, sustainable transportation that reduces traffic and pollution.

Project mission: Our goal is to apply data analytics tools to be able to observe the evolution of the Ecobici network and provide recommendations for its future development and prosperity.

The analysis: We analyzed demographic and route data, which you can browse in the “Visualizations” tab. You can read more about the data sources and the analysis process in the “Data” tab.

What you can do:

  1. Explore. We invite you to explore the data yourself, have fun, and come to your own conclusion about how the Ecobici network can continue to make this city even more beautiful and accessible.
  2. Become a rider! Don't have your ecobici membership yet? Sign up here: https://www.ecobici.cdmx.gob.mx/es/registro/inicio
